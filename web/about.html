Core Engine

ICT/SMC Signal Engine

A full state-machine strategy engine based on Inner Circle Trader methodology. Every trade entry is gated by a Break of Structure event, followed by identification of a valid Order Block, then filtered through a multi-factor confluence score. Minimum score of 3 required before any execution.

Runs exclusively on the M15 timeframe during active kill zone sessions — London open and New York open — where institutional order flow is highest.

Risk Management

Built to protect capital first

Every position is sized using the exact ATR stop distance — not a fixed pip amount. This means lot size adapts to volatility automatically. At 2% risk per trade, position sizing is always correct regardless of market condition.

Break-even logic moves the stop loss to entry price (+1 pip spread) the moment the trade reaches 1R profit — turning every early winner into a risk-free trade. A daily drawdown circuit breaker halts all activity if equity drops 5% from session open.

Validation

Three-mode backtesting

Results aren't cherry-picked — all performance metrics come from out-of-sample data. The 70/30 split backtest trains parameters on the first 70% of historical data and tests on the remaining 30% with zero look-ahead bias.

Walk-forward testing goes further — rolling optimization windows ensure the strategy adapts correctly without overfitting. All 7 symbols pass all three backtesting modes with positive profit factor.
