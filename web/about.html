About EURUSD Trading Bot

An educational project exploring trend-following strategies, backtesting methodologies, and quantitative trading analysis.

Project Overview

EURUSD Trading Bot is a specialized algorithmic trading system developed to demonstrate professional backtesting practices, parameter optimization, and forex strategy development on the EURUSD M15 timeframe.

The system implements a proven EMA crossover strategy with RSI confirmation, a professional-grade backtesting engine using real MT5 data, grid search optimization across 64+ parameter combinations, and comprehensive risk management protocols.

This project combines quantitative finance principles with software engineering best practices, demonstrating data-driven decision making, statistical analysis, and system architecture design for automated trading systems.

Research & Methodology

Evidence-based strategy development and testing

01

Strategy Research

Extensive research into proven trading methodologies including Grid Trading, Inner Circle Trader concepts, Mean Reversion, and High-Frequency Scalping techniques.

02

Parameter Optimization

Systematic parameter sweeps across multiple dimensions to identify optimal configurations. Tested 1000+ parameter combinations to maximize risk-adjusted returns.

03

Backtesting Validation

Rigorous backtesting on historical data spanning multiple market conditions. Walk-forward analysis and out-of-sample testing to avoid overfitting.

04

Risk Analysis

Comprehensive risk metrics including Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, profit factor, and win rate. Monte Carlo simulations for robustness testing.
