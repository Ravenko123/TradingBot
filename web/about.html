About Zenith Trading Bot

An algorithmic trading platform with multi-mode backtesting, real-time dashboards, and MT5 integration.

Project Overview

Zenith Trading Bot is an algorithmic trading system built around ICT/SMC (Inner Circle Trader / Smart Money Concepts) methodology. It detects Break of Structure events, identifies Order Block zones, and enters pullback trades with multi-factor confluence scoring on the M15 timeframe.

The system implements a state-machine signal engine with 7 confluence factors (BOS displacement, FVG, liquidity sweeps, session kill zones, premium/discount zones, rejection wicks, OB strength), break-even trailing, and ATR-based risk management. All signals are validated with split-sample backtesting and walk-forward analysis on real MT5 data.

This project combines quantitative finance principles with software engineering best practices, demonstrating data-driven decision making, statistical analysis, and system architecture design for automated trading systems.

Research & Methodology

Evidence-based strategy development and testing

01

Strategy Research

In-depth study of ICT/SMC methodology — Break of Structure, Order Blocks, Fair Value Gaps, liquidity sweeps, premium/discount zones, and kill zone session timing. Implemented as a multi-bar state machine with confluence scoring.

02

Parameter Optimization

Systematic parameter sweeps across multiple dimensions to identify optimal configurations. Tested 1000+ parameter combinations to maximize risk-adjusted returns.

03

Backtesting Validation

Rigorous backtesting on historical data spanning multiple market conditions. Walk-forward analysis and out-of-sample testing to avoid overfitting.

04

Risk Analysis

Comprehensive risk metrics including Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, profit factor, and win rate. Monte Carlo simulations for robustness testing.
