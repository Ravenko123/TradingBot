Backtest Tips

01
Use 60+ Days
Longer periods give more statistically significant results and reveal how the strategy handles different market regimes.
02
Test Multiple Pairs
A robust strategy performs well across correlated and uncorrelated instruments. Try EURUSD, XAUUSD, and NAS100.
03
Watch the Drawdown
Win rate alone means nothing — focus on max drawdown and profit factor. Aim for PF > 1.5 and DD < 15%.

Monte Carlo Simulation

Stress-test your strategy by shuffling trade order across 200 iterations to gauge outcome stability

What it does

Takes your backtest trades and randomly re-orders them 200 times, producing 200 different equity curves from the same trades.

Why it matters

Shows the range of possible outcomes. A tight distribution means consistent results regardless of trade order — a loose one signals luck-dependency.

Key metrics

P10 (worst 10%), P50 (median), P90 (best 10%). If P10 is still profitable, your strategy is genuinely robust.
